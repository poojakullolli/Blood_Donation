Donation Guidelines

Pre-Donation

Eat iron-rich foods, stay hydrated, and get adequate rest before donating blood.

Post-Donation Care

Rest for 15 minutes, avoid heavy lifting for 24 hours, and drink plenty of fluids.

Donation Frequency

Whole blood can be donated every 56 days. Platelets can be donated every 7 days.
